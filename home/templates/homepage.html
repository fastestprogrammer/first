About Us

Intelligent system to survey roads in fast and efficient way.

We are using AI for video processing for anomalies detection on roads. Our system is able to detect and classify different types of road anomalies.

Our main focus is to make this system more and more efficient so that we can make roads anomaly free for everyone.

Process

Our Product background and functioning

1

Record road survey data

First step is to collect data of road for survey.

2

AI processing of recording

Process the recorded video with AI model to find anomalies.

3

Anomaly classification

Classify the anomaly if it is pothole, patch or crack.

4

Identify condition of pothole

Identify the condition of pothole and mark as red, yellow or green.

5

Data to Dashboard

After processing and analysing the videos displays results to dashboard

6

Ticket raise

Raise a ticket for range of potholes to assign to some authority.

7

Ticket assignment

Assign the ticket to some authorised person to fix anomalies.

8

Fix pothole

Team go to the location and fix the anomalies.

9

Click images of fix and close ticket

Once the anomalies fixed, team needs to upload images of fixed potholes.
